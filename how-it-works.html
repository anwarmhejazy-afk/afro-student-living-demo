Student journey

From search to confirmation

A clearer and safer process designed to help students find accommodation without confusion, pressure, or blind payment.

🔍
Step 01

Search accommodation

Browse rooms, hostels, studios, and apartments by location, university area, property type, and budget.

🏠
Step 02

Compare verified listings

Review photos, prices, features, location details, and listing information before deciding what fits your needs.

🖼️
Step 03

Open full property details

View the property page, check the gallery, understand the yearly price, and confirm key facilities.

📩
Step 04

Request viewing or booking

Send a request before committing. Students can ask to view the property or start a booking request through admin.

🛡️
Step 05

Admin reviews the request

Afro Student Living admin checks the request, confirms availability, and helps reduce unsafe or unclear arrangements.

✅
Step 06

Confirm next steps

Once details are confirmed, the student receives guidance before moving forward with payment, viewing, or move-in plans.

For property owners

How property submission works

Property owners can submit accommodation details, but every listing remains admin-controlled before it goes live.

1

Submit property details

Owners submit the property name, location, price, description, photos, and contact details.

2

Admin review

The admin checks the property details before deciding whether it should be listed publicly.

3

Approved listing goes live

Once approved, the accommodation can appear on the public listings page for students to discover.
